Positions

In cheerleading, there are many different positions, inlcuding backspot, main base, side base, and flyer. Let's look more deeply into these positions.

Backspots: Backspots are initially the backbone of the stunt, they are there to make it easier for the bases, to help the flyer feel secure, but mostly to be there to catch. Their job is to hold the flyers ankles and lift up as much as possible releasing weight from the bases hands.
