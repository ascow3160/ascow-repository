CHEERLEADING

Let's learn about a world full of cheerleading!

In cheerleading, you get to spread school spirit, make lasting friendships, and even compete for awards.

Cheerleading is a sport that involves spreading school spirit, making lasting relationships, and overall being physically active. In this sport, you will cheer on other sports, some include basketball and football. You get to have cheers and dances that you will do during the games to get the crowd pumped up and to encourage the team. You also learn how to stunt depending on the level of cheerleading you are at. Stunting is when you have 4 people in a group, one flyer, two bases, and one backspot. Bases and backspots will hold the flyers in the air and do cool tricks in front of the crowds. You can even compete in cheer, you go to different places and show off your routines and compete with other schools for awards based on your skills.
